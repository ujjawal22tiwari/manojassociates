Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

From a humble beginning in Lucknow to a pan-India infrastructure powerhouse — a decade and a half of building the future.

2009

Company Founded

Manoj Associates established in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh as a proprietorship firm with a vision to serve India's growing infrastructure sector.

2012

First Metro Project

Secured and executed civil construction works for Lucknow Metro Stations under Gulermak-Tata JV — marking our entry into metro rail infrastructure.

2015

National Expansion

Extended operations to Delhi and Pune, executing tunnel segment casting for Delhi Metro DC-09 and Pune Metro finishing works across multiple stations.

2018

Tunneling Expertise

Established deep specialization in precast tunnel segment ring casting — working on GMLR (Goregaon-Mulund Link Road) and Patna Metro projects.

2021

Aviation Sector Entry

Awarded prestigious flooring contract for Noida International Airport by Tata Projects Ltd — expanding into India's booming aviation infrastructure sector.

2023

Pan-India Presence

Active projects in Chennai, Bhopal, Patna, Palghar, Pune and Lucknow simultaneously — cementing a true pan-India footprint across 20+ cities.

2025

20Cr+ Turnover & Growing

Achieved annual turnover of 20Cr+ with 50+ landmark projects executed. Continuing to build the arteries of modern India with unwavering commitment.
